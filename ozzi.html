I collaborated with Ozzi Art & Decor, a Kosovo-based startup specializing in handcrafted metal furniture, including custom coffee tables. I was responsible for building their brand identity, managing social media, designing ad campaigns, and creating engaging content. I also directed product photoshoots to highlight the craftsmanship and modern aesthetic of their pieces, helping to establish a strong and cohesive online presence.
