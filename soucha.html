THE BRAND

Country Kosovo
Branch Handmade Soaps
Duration 2 Months

I worked with Soucha, a home studio creating handcrafted soaps using natural, bio ingredients. I developed their visual identity, using florals and petals as the foundation of the branding. The logo was inspired by the organic shape of their soaps, while the color palette—centered on soft pinks and whites—was designed to be flexible, adapting to each soap’s unique ingredients and hues. I also directed and photographed product shoots, choosing natural settings to reflect the brand’s essence and values.
